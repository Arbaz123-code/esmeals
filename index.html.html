About ES Meals

ES Meals is a brand of taste by Endurance Superia. ES Meals provide the best tiffin meals in Mumbai.

ES Meals was founded in June 2020 during the difficult phase of the lockdown during the Covid-19 pandemic with intentions of:

  • Addressing the food requirements of people trapped in the lockdown.
  • Providing a means of employment to maids & cooks who faced livelihood issues due to unemployment.

Proudly, we can say that we have been able to address these points very passionately & wholeheartedly.

Today, ES Meals has a new horizon of intentions to serve tasty & hygienic food to entire Mumbai.
